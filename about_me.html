Favorite Restaurant

Oceanolwb Restaurant

This restaurant is my favorite because of its fresh ingredients, frequently updated menu, and the fact that a friend is the head chef. The personal touch and quality ingredients make dining there a true pleasure.

Favorite Food: Saltimbocca alla Romana

Saltimbocca alla Romana is a dish I love to serve. I typically pair it with a light salad and a crisp white wine so that the delicate flavors of veal, prosciutto, and sage are the stars of the meal.
